How to Get Started?

Create Your Account

Fill out your account with some basic information, including your residence, workplace, and working hours.

Meet Your Driver

Use our chat to get to know your driver. You’ll be picked up at your regular location or agree on an alternative pick-up location

Enjoy Your Journey

Get in, sit back and enjoy the journey that takes you directly to your destination with some free cup of coffee from us.
