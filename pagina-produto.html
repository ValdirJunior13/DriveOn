How to Get Started?

Create Your Account

Fill out your account with some basic information, including your residence, workplace, and working hours.

Plan Your Trips

Open the app, click on the "Schedule" tab, and enter your working hours for the upcoming week. Then, you’ll see a list of our available drivers.

Meet Your Driver

Use the chat to get to know your driver. You’ll be picked up at your usual location, or you can arrange an alternative meeting point.

Enjoy Your Ride

Hop in the car, relax, and enjoy the journey directly to your destination, with complimentary coffee from us.
